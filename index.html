beta.vifi.ee

Wireframe • UI/UX design • Frontend development • Graphic design • Testing

Vifi is a leading VOD service in Estonia. Operated since 2010, Vifi has over 10,000 titles in its collection, ranging from movie to TV series. In 2014, it launches an application for Samsung Smart TV - the first of its kind in the Baltic region. I am responsible for re-designing Vifi's web interface as well as establishing a uniform brand recognation accross different platforms.

A five years old interface

Vifi's layout was very outdated, partly because they could not predict how fast can the service grow in just a couple of years. The structure of the website is very simple, almost too simple that it cannot host all the new and upcoming features. As Vifi grew, the layout became more and more inconsistent. Besides, the website is not responsive. It's just the matter of time until the company's owners realized that they need a better user interface.

Not just better...

The first thing I knew that I should do with Vifi is, of course, to deliver our visitor a more modern experience. It should be friendly enough for everybody, from a school girl to a retired grandma, to be able to use. Besides, since there are more people watching videos on their mobile devices, responsiveness is one of the key points to boost the service's accessibility. Last but not least, the layout should also be flexible and easy to update.

Search. Pay. Watch.

Because Vifi has a very extensive library, I understood that we have to focus on making the search function work really well so that users can find what they want quickly. In the new design, besides searching by keywords, users can also filter the result based on category, release date and length of the movie. The filter bar looks extremely minimalistic. Users can switch betweens tabs by clicking or swifting their fingers on a touch screen. As soon as they tick a check box, the result list will be shorterned down immediately. The whole experience feel effortlessly smooth.

A seamless experience across different devices

In 2014, Vifi partnered with Samsung to launch the first VOD application for smart TV in Estonia. The app should explore the uniqueness of smart TV's OS, and at the same time reflect Vifi's signature appearance. Designing UI for TV is very challenging. The different screen sizes require a whole new approach to layout construction. User's behavior is also different. Instead of mouse or touch screen, users interact with the devices through remote control and gestures. Thus, I needed to re-think the whole concept for the UI. There are four main points:

Navigation

Instead of clicking, users navigates in smart TV through the hotkeys: up, down, left and right. Our navigation should be categorized according to this.

Readability

Although the screen size of the TV is bigger, but the actual effective area for content is much less. People don't want to read a lot of info on a TV screen.

Simplicification

We could not bring everything from the desktop version to the TV one, at least in the first release. Basic features must feel great to use.

Continuity

The TV app does not stand alone, nor do the desktop and mobile apps. The users want to switch between devices without any interuption.

Finding the right balance

Midaxo's target customers are corporate and businessmen who are very busy, so we need to design the platform in a way that all important information can be easily accessible within a blink of an eye. However, we must differ ourselves from other die-hard corporate softwares in a market. The user interface should be modern, interactive, but not too complicated. Every gesture such as drag-and-drop, zoom should be carefully planned to ensure convenience.

User interface is not only a part of the product. It is the product.

At Midaxo, we understand that a good user interface is the one that enables our users to discover the platform from A to Z without any difficulties. It is not something that we applied at the end of the development process to make things prettier. Planning for user experience took place at the very beginning. It is the glue that keep every features, plug-ins, widgets together. Thanks to this, our platform's user interface has received numberous compliments from our customers as well as external audits.
