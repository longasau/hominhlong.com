Beyond a beautiful interface...

Vifi

Lead UI/UX Designer

I am responsible for re-designing Vifi's web interface as well as establishing a uniform brand recognation accross different platforms.

BooxTV

booxdemo.aminocom.com/html5_amino/

UI/UX Designer

BooxTV is a platform for TV service providers to stream their programs on different types of devices, from mobiles, tablets to Set-Top-Boxes. As the main UI/UX Designer, I was in charge to design a common interface that can be easily customized and modulized to specific clients, yet easy to take on for end users.

Highcharts

www.highcharts.com

UI/UX Designer

Highcharts is a charting library written in pure JavaScript, offering an easy way of adding interactive charts to your web site or web application. I was working for Highsoft - the great company behind Highcharts - as a freelancer from 2015 to 2017. I helped them revamp their website along with their virtual shop. Later, I participate in developing the UI for Highcharts Editor, a JavaScript/CSS library for Highcharts 5 which enables non-programmers to easily create and publish charts

Midaxo
